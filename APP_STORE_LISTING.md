## Description

Pilot your ship through the VOID RIFT — an endless cosmic warzone where every wave hits harder than the last.

VOID RIFT is a fast-paced arcade survival shooter with roguelike upgrades, procedural enemies, and one simple goal: don't die.

FEATURES:
- Intense wave-based combat with 8 distinct enemy types
- Massive boss battles with multi-phase AI
- 4 weapon types: Normal, Laser, Spread Shot, and Homing Missiles
- Level-up system with randomized upgrade choices each level
- Persistent shop upgrades that carry between runs
- 3 unlockable ship skins
- 4 biome environments that change every 10 waves
- Achievement system with 8 milestones
- Daily Challenge mode with seeded runs
- Procedural FM-synthesized audio and music
- One-thumb controls designed for mobile

CONTROLS:
- Drag the left side of the screen to move
- Tap the right side to dash (you're invincible while dashing!)
- Your ship fires automatically

Built for quick sessions. No ads. No paywalls. Just pure arcade action.
